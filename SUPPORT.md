# Support

## How to Get Help

- **Questions & Discussions** — Use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/askend-lab/hak-public/discussions) for general questions, ideas, and community conversations.
- **Bug Reports** — Open a [Bug Report](https://github.com/askend-lab/hak-public/issues/new?template=bug_report.md) issue with steps to reproduce.
- **Feature Requests** — Open a [Feature Request](https://github.com/askend-lab/hak-public/issues/new?template=feature_request.md) issue describing your use case.
- **Security Issues** — See [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). **Do not** open a public issue for security vulnerabilities.

## Before Opening an Issue

1. Search [existing issues](https://github.com/askend-lab/hak-public/issues) to avoid duplicates.
2. Check [Discussions](https://github.com/askend-lab/hak-public/discussions) — your question may already be answered.
3. Include your environment details (OS, Node.js version, browser).
